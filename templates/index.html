Our Mission

We meet willingness with support

The Meet You There Foundation exists to support individuals and families who have demonstrated a willingness to take meaningful steps toward healing, recovery, growth, and stability — but who lack the financial resources or access needed to move forward.

Healing & recovery

Support for mental health, sobriety, and the daily work of getting well — meeting people exactly where they are.

Growth & transformation

Coaching, education, and resilience-building for people committed to becoming who they're ready to be.

Stability & family

Transitional assistance and family education that help a person — and the people around them — find solid ground.

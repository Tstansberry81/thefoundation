Our Mission

We meet people where they are

The Meet You There Foundation exists to support individuals and families who have demonstrated a willingness to take meaningful steps toward healing, recovery, growth, stability, or personal transformation — but who lack the financial resources, support, or access needed to move forward.

We believe lasting change cannot be forced, purchased, or imposed. It begins when a person raises their hand, asks for help, and chooses to engage in their own journey. Our role is not to create willingness, but to meet it.

Guiding philosophy

When people are ready to take the next step, they should not have to do it alone.

The Meet You There Foundation seeks to remove barriers for individuals who have demonstrated commitment to their own growth and recovery — providing financial assistance, educational opportunities, coaching support, therapeutic resources, and other forms of practical aid that help them move forward.
